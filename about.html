About This Project

Hogwarts: Houses & Wizards is a fan-made microsite celebrating the lore, characters, and values of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Project Purpose

This educational fan experience brings together curated summaries, house histories, and wizard profiles in a single, accessible guide. It emphasizes respectful homage, easy navigation, and a spoiler-conscious design.

Credits & Use

Fonts are served via Google Fonts: Cinzel Decorative for headings and Inter for body text. Interface icons rely on semantic CSS rather than external icon packs to keep the experience performant. Imagery uses lightweight WebP placeholders suitable for rapid prototyping.
