About This Fan Project

Hogwarts Houses & Wizards began as a design-to-code conversion challenge and grew into a curated reference celebrating the lore of the wizarding world.

Project Description

This mini-site reimagines a multi-page template as an immersive Hogwarts resource. It blends a cohesive dark theme, data-driven content, spoiler tooling, and accessible navigation so fans can explore houses, characters, and key events with ease.

Every page is powered by a shared data model and modular scripts to keep information consistent. Cards, modals, and filters adapt fluidly across devices, while subtle magical accents evoke the wonder of the wizarding world.
