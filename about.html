Behind the project

About This Fan Site

Hogwarts: Houses & Wizards is a fan-made exploration of the castle's lore, designed to keep spoilers transparent and the magic of discovery intact.

Project Blurb

This mini-site celebrates Hogwarts' enduring stories through data-driven profiles, accessible design, and a spoiler-conscious approach. It was crafted as a learning exercise in semantic HTML, modern CSS, and progressive enhancement with vanilla JavaScript.
